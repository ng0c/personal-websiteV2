About me

I'm Anita and I am from Norway. I moved to London in 2016 to complete an undergraduate degree in Psychology. After completing my degree, I wasn't sure if I wanted to work or do further studies in the field of Psychology so I decided to take a year break from education and work as a Data Analyst. During this year, I also completed a free 8-weeks course with Code First Girls on Web Development where I learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and JQuery. This was when I realised that I really enjoyed coding and wanted to learn more about it. Since then I have created a few websites, one of them was for my friend's startup which is a service run by medical students, helping other students get into medical school.

All of this led me to a MSc Computer Science course at University of Bristol. I hope to learn a lot this year and by the end pursue a career as a Software Engineer.
